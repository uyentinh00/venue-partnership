about us

about
lucky tiles

lucky tiles is a modern mahjong social club creating casual, welcoming events for young adults across major cities. our sessions feel approachable, stylish, and low-pressure — whether someone is completely new to mahjong or already obsessed.

we bring together people over a shared game — and through that, we're building something bigger: a genuine cross-cultural community where connection happens naturally.

the opportunity

why mahjong
works for venues

unlike loud nightlife or one-off events, mahjong creates a warm, conversational atmosphere that complements bars, restaurants, cat cafés, sports clubs, and any social space.

⏱

longer dwell time & more orders

mahjong sessions run 2–3 hours naturally — players settle in, order multiple rounds of food and drinks, and stay far longer than a typical visit.

👥

group bookings & repeat visits

players come in groups of 4, filling tables efficiently. regulars come back every session and often revisit your venue outside of event nights.

🍻

real F&B revenue

a social game pairs naturally with eating and drinking. our events consistently drive food and beverage spending throughout the entire session.

📸

organic reach & community buzz

players photograph and share the experience on instagram and tiktok. every event generates free content and word-of-mouth for your venue.
